How to Play

Exiles are powered by Polygon blockchain. You can add Polygon chain to Metamask by following this steps. If you don't know what is Polygon or how to bridge your assets you can check official webpage and guide from here

Connect

Connect your wallet by hitting "Connect" button on the top of the page.

You can mint new Exile by hitting "Mint" button or buy one from Opensea.
